Original Bihar-inspired open-world action prototype

Bihar Streets

Cruise through a fictional Patna-inspired district, pass the ghats, station lanes, markets, and farmland, and complete local delivery missions in a retro crime-drama sandbox style.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys move
  • Shift sprint / accelerate
  • E enter or exit the auto-rickshaw
  • Space interact with mission markers
  • M toggle mini-map

This is not a clone or replica of any commercial game; it is an original browser-playable homage with Bihar-themed locations, characters, and missions.
